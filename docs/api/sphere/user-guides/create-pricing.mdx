# ➕ Create a Pricing

## 0. Requisites

:::warning
To upload a pricing to SPHERE, you must have a **registered account**.
:::

## 1. Select Upload Pricing option

Once you have logged into your account, click on the avatar located in the top-right corner of your screen:

<figure style={{textAlign: "center"}}>
    <img src={CreatePricingStep0} />
    <figcaption style={{fontStyle: "italic"}}>Main page</figcaption>
</figure>

Select *Upload Pricing* action in the modal:

<figure style={{textAlign: "center"}}>
    <img src={AccountOptions} />
    <figcaption style={{fontStyle: "italic"}}>Account actions</figcaption>
</figure>

## 2. Pick your pricing

A modal will appear prompting you to upload a Pricing2Yaml file. Once you have selected a
Pricing2Yaml file on your computer click on **Submit File** button:

<figure style={{textAlign: "center"}}>
    <img src={UploadPricingModal} />
    <figcaption style={{fontStyle: "italic"}}>Submit pricing modal</figcaption>
</figure>


## 3. Visualize your pricing

If everything went well, click the avatar icon again and select **My Pricings**, you’ll see your newly created pricing listed there:

<figure style={{textAlign: "center"}}>
    <img src={PricingUploaded} />
    <figcaption style={{fontStyle: "italic"}}>Pricing registered</figcaption>
</figure>

:::info
Pricings can be organized into [collections](create-collection.mdx). When you upload a pricing, it isn’t automatically assigned to any collection. You’ll need to manually assign your pricing to a specific collection.
:::

If you click inside the pricing card you will see a dashboard with information about the
pricing. See the **Pricing card** section for more details.
